## Dimensionality Reduction 
UMAP, PCA, T-SNE

## Neighborhood Graph Construction
Build a k-nearest-neighbor graph for clustering and visualization from PCA or latent.

## Clustering
Identify groups of similar cells (e.g. cell types or states) in an unsupervised way, leiden or louvain algorithms.


## Trajectory and Pseudotime Analysis
Model continuous developmental or differentiation processes.

## Differential Expression and Marker Gene Detection
Identify genes that are characteristic of clusters or conditions.


## Gene Signature Scoring
Quantify the expression of gene sets per cell.

## Visualization
Plot results (embeddings, marker expression, QC) to interpret data.
